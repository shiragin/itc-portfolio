Why I enjoy coding

I've always loved learning new things, and I've dabbled in building websites ever since my first Geocities websites, complete with terrible background images and blinking green text. However, it wasn't until last year that I've started my coding learning path with Python. That was also when I've discovered how much I enjoy it.

The structural and organisational skills required of a developer appeal to me, but more than that, I enjoy the immense creativity of the problem-solving process. Writing code challenges you to face problems that may seem insurmountable at first, and to break them into smaller, more manageable problems. It is this part that I find the most rewarding - when the problem doesn't seem so overwhelming anymore, along with that beautiful, blessed moment when everything clicks together and your solution finally works!
