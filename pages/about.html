Why I love coding.

I've always enjoyed learning new things, about history and arts and different languages. However, even though I've built websites since I was a kid (my very own Geocities websites, complete with terrible background images and blinking green text), it wasn't until last year that I've started learning coding seriously, starting with Python. That was also when I've discovered how much I enjoy it.

The methodicality and oranisational skills required of a coder appeal to me, but more than that I enjoy the creativity of the problem-solving process. Writing code challenges you to face a problem that may seem insurmountable at first, and find a way to break it into smaller, more manageable problems. It is this part that I find the most rewarding - when the problem doesn't seem so overwhelming anymore, and that beautiful, blessed moment when everything clicks together and your solution finally works!
